Brief Bio

I'm Gautham Krishna Gudur, and I work as a Data Scientist at Ericsson R&D, where I work with the Global AI Accelerator (GAIA) team. I also do independent research at an intersection of Machine Learning (Deep, Uncertain, On-device) and Ubiqitous Computing. You can find more about me here.

Research Interests
My research interests span multiple domains, and I would like to have an interdisciplinary aspect to the same.
