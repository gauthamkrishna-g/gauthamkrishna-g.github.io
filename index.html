Brief Bio

I'm Gautham Krishna Gudur, and I work as a Data Scientist at Ericsson R&D, where I work with the Global AI Accelerator (GAIA) team. I also do independent research in Machine Learning (Deep, Uncertain, on-device) and Ubiqitous & Wearable Computing. You can find more about me here.

My research interests span an intersection of multiple domains, and I would like to have an interdisciplinary touch to the same.
