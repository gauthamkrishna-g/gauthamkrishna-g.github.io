Brief Bio

Hello! I'm Gautham Krishna Gudur, and I work as a Data Scientist at Ericsson R&D, in the Global AI Accelerator (GAIA) team. I also do independent research at an intersection of machine learning and ubiqitous computing. My current research theme is Data-efficient AI. Previously, I worked at an AI assisted wearable health-care startup called SmartCardia (a spin-off from EPFL, Switzerland). You can find more about me here.

Research Interests
My research interests span across multiple domains, and I would like to have an interdisciplinary aspect to the same.

Keywords that might best describe my present and past interests:

Machine (Deep, On-Device/Resource-Constrained, Bayesian, Incremental/Continual, Active, Federated, Meta, Reinforcement) Learning, Data Efficient AI, Ubiquitous Computing, Activity Recognition, Computer Vision, Audio Sensing, Pervasive Healthcare, AI4SocialGood, NLP, IoT, Time-series Analysis, Anomaly Detection
