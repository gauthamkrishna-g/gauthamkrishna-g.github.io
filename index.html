Brief Bio

I am currently a Data Scientist at Ericsson R&D, in the Global AI Accelerator (GAIA) team. I also do independent research broadly at an intersection of Machine Learning and Ubiqitous Computing. My current research theme is Resource-Efficient AI. Previously, I worked as a Machine Learning Engineer at an AI assisted wearable health-care startup called SmartCardia (a spin-off from EPFL, Switzerland).

In a past life, I was a Research Assistant at Solarillion Foundation, and I earned a Bachelor's in Information Technology from SSN College of Engineering, Chennai. You can find more about me here, and about my work experience here.

Research Interests
My research interests span across multiple domains with a good mix of theory and applied ML, and I would like to have an interdisciplinary aspect to the same.

Keywords that might best describe my present and past interests (and hopefully in the future, who knows!):

Machine (Deep, On-Device/Resource-Constrained, Bayesian, Incremental/Continual, Active, Federated, Meta, Reinforcement) Learning, Resource/Data-Efficient AI, Ubiquitous Computing, Activity Recognition, Computer Vision, Audio Sensing, Pervasive Healthcare, AI4SocialGood, NLP, IoT, Time-series Analysis, Anomaly Detection, Telecom
